Welcome

This is the official website for the Spring 2016 Peer-led Biocomputing Group at UT Austin, offered through the Center for Computational Biology and Bioinformatics. The goal of this class is to take away any fear of programming that you may have so that computing can become an advantage rather than a barrier to your research.

The class will meet on Wednesdays from 4-5 pm in FNT 1.104. In addition, feel free to join us for Open Coding Hour at Tuesdays 5-6pm in CCBB conference room (GDC 7.514) for any extra help or feedback.

See links on left side of page for contact information and helpful resources.

Installation and Setup Instructions

Please carefully read and follow the setup instructions provided in the link for your operating system: either Mac or PC (if you are on Linux, the Mac instructions should work for you). It is critical that you have your computer properly set up, with any necessary software or operating system installed, by the second week of class (1/27/16). Do not save installation to the last minute - it may take several hours (esp. PC), and you may encounter difficulties!! If you need help, please come to Open Coding Hour!
